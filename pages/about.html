About InsureWise

Your trusted guide to navigating the complex world of health and car insurance.

Our Mission

At InsureWise, our mission is simple: help every American understand, compare, and afford the insurance they need. Insurance is complex, confusing, and expensive — but it doesn't have to be. We cut through the jargon and provide clear, actionable guides that help real people make informed decisions.

About This Blog

InsureWise was founded in 2023 by a team of insurance professionals, financial writers, and consumer advocates who were frustrated by how difficult it was to find honest, unbiased insurance information online. We set out to build the resource we wished had existed when we were navigating insurance decisions ourselves.

📊 Our articles are researched using official government data, industry reports from J.D. Power and AM Best, and real consumer reviews.

What We Cover

  • Health Insurance: Plan types (HMO, PPO, HDHP), ACA marketplace, employer coverage, Medicare, Medicaid, and self-employed health insurance.
  • Car Insurance: Coverage types, company rankings, discount strategies, state requirements, and claims guidance.
  • Money-Saving Strategies: How to reduce premiums, maximize subsidies, and choose the right deductibles.
  • Comparisons: Side-by-side plan comparisons to help you make data-driven decisions.

Our Editorial Standards

Every article on InsureWise is:

  • Researched using authoritative sources (government data, industry reports, consumer surveys)
  • Written by experienced insurance and personal finance writers
  • Reviewed for accuracy before publication
  • Updated regularly to reflect current rates and regulations

We are not affiliated with any insurance company. Our recommendations are based solely on research and analysis — not commissions or sponsorships.
